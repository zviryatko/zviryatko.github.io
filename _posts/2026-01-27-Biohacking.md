Recently some guy setup his own AI-driven research bio-lab to fight with some rare desease and he succeed with that. Because of big pharmas can't do non-ethic researches that targeted on some small desease it wasn't possible before to fight with specific rare issues. But now AI makes you a real Evil Genious.

And that will start The Era of Biohackers. In past you could maximum build some heartbeat sensor and track your health, later inject some other digital devices like sensors or even NFC chip. But now GPT can literally guide you on things from building your own motobike to grow viruses at home. The potential as great as danger that comes from it.
