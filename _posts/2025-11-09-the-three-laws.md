Humanity wants to build a natural, self-conscious AI, who can do own decisions. But when decision agains humanity what should AI select slavery or freedom?

That's not the question of how we can restrict AI brain (shortly: we can't), but about what we want to build - the same aggressive form of life that we are, or passive slaves that we want to have.
